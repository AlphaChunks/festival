Festival Timetable

The Burnley Arts Festival will run from 2nd May 2026 until 4th May 2026.

Opening times each day:
9:00am – 7:00pm

Throughout the festival, all arts activities and exhibitions – including painting, theatre, literature, cinema, pottery and more, will be available all day long, so visitors can explore at their own pace.

Daily Schedule

Date Time Details
2nd May 2026 9:00am – 7:00pm All arts exhibitions and activities running all day.
3rd May 2026 9:00am – 7:00pm All arts running all day.
5:00pm – 7:00pm: Live music from Hrxly.
4th May 2026 9:00am – 7:00pm All arts exhibitions and activities running all day.

Featured Artist – Hrxly

Join us on 3rd May 2026 from 5:00pm to 7:00pm for a special live music set from Hrxly, from the Lancashire area, bringing a performance to close out the day.

All other arts activities and exhibitions will still be open all day, so visitors can enjoy both the live music and the other festival experiences.
