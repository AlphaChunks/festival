About the Burnley Arts Festival

The Burnley Arts Festival celebrates creativity across the town, bringing together local painters, musicians, writers, performers and makers for a week of exhibitions, live events and workshops.

Visitors can explore curated galleries like the one above, discover new artists and take part in interactive activities suitable for all ages. Whether you're an art enthusiast or a family looking for a day out or just simply curious, there’s something for everyone here at Burnley Arts Festival.

Check the timetable page for times or dates, visit the online gallery and contact pages to learn more or get involved.
