About the Festival

The Burnley Arts Festival is an annual celebration of heritage, creativity, culture and community. Featuring artwork, live performances, music, literature, theatre, film and hands on workshops, the festival aims to show the incredible talent found across Burnley and the surrounding areas.

Each year, artists of all ages and backgrounds are invited to exhibit their work, host sessions and enjoy all of the arts themselves. The festival brings together students, families, professionals and local groups to enjoy a week full of colour, imagination and creative expression.

Our mission is simple: make art accessible to all and to inspire creativity in everyone, whether you’re an experienced artist, a complete beginner or simply someone is creative minded.

Our Vision

The Burnley Arts Festival encourages people to explore new forms of creativity and supports local talent by giving them a professional platform to showcase their work.

We aim to:

  • Promote local artists and creative businesses.
  • Provide community workshops and educational opportunities.
  • Celebrate cultural diversity and artistic expression.
  • Offer family-friendly activities for all ages.
  • Strengthen Burnley’s identity as a vibrant creative hub.
